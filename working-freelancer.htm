about oakwave

We are a small, resourceful, and fierce group of realistic creatives who deliver marketing strategy, marketing review, decision-making, and on-demand CMO-as-a-service solutions for B2B brands.

We service all industries, but we specialize in B2B (though we have had MANY B2C clients as well, it’s just not our core strength). We have a penchant for franchise sales, SaaS, medical, and manufacturing. But, only because we’ve had clusters of clients in each of those areas. As long as the nature of the challenge is marketing, sales, or business, we’re specialized there. This requires a lot more work on our part, but we know your industry like our own by the end of our process.

More on industry specialization
What's it like to work with OAKWAVE?

Our typical process kicks-off when a client approaches us with a growth challenge or problem. They may not fully understand that problem, but they know they have one. After we get to know each other a bit, we typically engage one of two ways. With a strategy, we do a full audit and deep-dive into the challenge and create a roadmap for marketing success for the next three years. The alternative path is a fixed block of hours to be depleted at the client's leisure. We find that most of our clients are most successful by starting with a strategy and following-up with a block of hours to assist with strategic implementation. We even publish our pricing information here, in a very un-agency like move.

MARKETING, SALES, AND GROWTH STRATEGY

Do you know how parents always say, "I wish there was a manual on parenting my kid!" That's kind of what our strategies are, except for your marketing, sales, and business growth. Go ahead, try to find a better marketing, sales, or growth strategy, it's right next to the Fountain of Youth and anything marketed as "free" that's actually free.

BRAND STRATEGY, POSITIONING, AND VOICE

Unifying your organization behind a single voice, position, slogan, and value proposition is critical to making efficient use of your marketing efforts. We make this process painless and actually fun. Your brand is your foundation and a weak foundation makes for a shaky house.

CONTENT BIRTH & REBIRTH

This is not your intern's SEO-packed, boring Encylopedia entry... Your content needs to grab the attention of your target market and hold onto it. This takes a special blend of sales, marketing, and wordsmithing. We believe a hundred amazing words will change the world for your business, not a hundred mediocre articles.

WEBSITE & MARTECH

The base of your marketing efforts Acting as the tracking epicenter of your marketing efforts, your website's form and function will make or break marketing. We assess, rethink, and offer tactical steps to lift impact of the homebase of your efforts.

Other Services

For the majority of our clients, we deliver a comprehensive, holistic marketing strategy (read: blueprints for three years of brand, marketing, and sales) followed by on-demand CMO-as-a-service consultation services delivered on a regular basis. For others, we offer turnkey execution and 100% project management services.
